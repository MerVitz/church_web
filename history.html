Our History

Explore the journey of our church through the years, from humble beginnings to a thriving community of faith and service.

1985 - Founding of the Church

Our church was founded with a small congregation of devoted believers committed to spreading the word of God.

1995 - First Church Building

After years of dedication and growth, we constructed our first church building to accommodate the growing congregation.

2005 - Outreach Programs

We expanded our mission beyond the church walls by initiating various outreach programs to support the community.

2015 - Youth and Women Ministries

Special ministries were introduced to empower the youth and women, fostering growth and leadership within the church.

2020 - Digital Transformation

We embraced technology by launching online services and social media outreach to connect with a broader audience.

2024 - Expansion and Future Vision

With God's grace, our church continues to grow, and we are planning new initiatives to further our mission globally.
